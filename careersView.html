CAREERS

PROJECT SUPERVISOR

JOB DESCRIPTION
1. Conduct daily toolbox talks with the workers as required by the Project Manager.
2. Supervise the construction work force and produce productivity requirement on the daily basis.
3. Report to Project Manager or Engineer any problem or issues or and other matters related to the workers at site.
4. Uphold safety standards being implement at the job site and report workers that are not improper PPE.
5. Maintain cleanliness and proper lock keeping in the workplace.

Benefits:
Additional leave
Health insurance
Opportunities for promotion
Paid training

Schedule:
8 hour shift

JOB REQUIREMENT:
College undergrad or graduate of any technical or vocational courses.
At least 2 years of construction job site experience.
Possess leadership quality, a good communicator, a problem solver, and ability to supervise workers.
Able to read construction plans and knowledge to execute the work according to the plans.

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent
