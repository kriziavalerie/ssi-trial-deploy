WHO WE ARE

SHEARWALL SYSTEMS INC. (SSI) is a design and build general contracting company.
We take pride in building only through shearwall and can convert
other structural designs into shearwall.

The company uses a monolithic approach in casting structural walls and slabs.
Work is programmed so that each cycle represents the completion of one floor,
one section, or one phase of the structure.
SSI follows a systematic construction procedure from the foundation to finishing.
Monolithic casting plus a systematic approach offers cost
savings, structural soundness, and faster project completion.
